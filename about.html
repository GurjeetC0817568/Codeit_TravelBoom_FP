About Us

Our History

Travel Boom Tours was created out of necessity: the need to provide exceptionally well-run tours with the best guides in the most comfortable vehicles, and with the best customer service around.

Our Mission

The mission is simple: become the best, most personalized tour company in world. The future of tourism is not with the large buses or one-size-fits-all group tours but the smaller and more personal touring.
